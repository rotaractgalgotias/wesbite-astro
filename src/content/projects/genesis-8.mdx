"Genesis" is an initiative dedicated to nurturing joy, companionship, and a sense of belonging among children at an orphanage school. Through heartfelt interactions, games, and conversations, volunteers from the Rotaract Club of Galgotias Educational Institutions created a day filled with love and laughter for these remarkable children. The essence of Genesis lies in the power of human connection to uplift vulnerable children and instill in them a sense of care, support, and belonging.

- "Genesis" unfolds at an orphanage school, providing children facing challenges with solace, support, and opportunities to dream.
- Volunteers from the Rotaract Club of Galgotias Educational Institutions embark on a mission to create a day filled with love, laughter, and meaningful connections for the children.
- Volunteers arrive armed with games, music, and enthusiasm, engaging the children in activities designed to spark joy and foster camaraderie.
- Through heartfelt interactions and conversations, volunteers seek to instill in the children a sense of care, support, love, and belonging.
- The initiative leaves an indelible mark on both the children and the volunteers, reminding them of the transformative power of love, companionship, and human connection.
- The memories of "Genesis" serve as a beacon of hope and inspiration, motivating volunteers to continue nurturing joy, companionship, and a sense of belonging wherever they go.
